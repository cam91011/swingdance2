Camille Hutt

Professional Swing Dancer

Camille has been dancing Lindy Hop and Charleston since 2007. She blends her movement with aspects of tap, ballet, and gymnastics. She has performed at various venues and events, including Lincoln Center's Midsummer Night Swing, Frankie 100, the Bix Beiderbecke Jazz Festival, Fashion's Night Out at Saks Fifth Avenue, and Columbia University. Camille's teaching focuses on connection, improv, and accessibility for all.
